About Us

Come experience our 18 hole course

The Kisii Sports Club is a family-oriented club founded in 1906 in Kisii town, Kenya. It is one of the oldest golf clubs in the country and the only members' club in Kisii, Nyamira, and Homa Bay counties. The club offers a wide range of amenities for children, young adults, adults, and seniors, including a 9-hole championship golf course, unique event grounds, swimming pool, state-of-the-art fitness gym, squash and tennis courts, snooker games, and accommodation. The club prides itself on its serene setting, surrounded by a rich variety of trees, indigenous forests, flowering shrubs, and fauna, providing a perfect ambiance for members to enjoy various activities.
